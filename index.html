How long do the Premium Lash Extensions typically last?

Our premium lash sets stay intact for roughly 4 to 6 weeks depending entirely on your natural lash growth cycle and care routines. We recommend scheduling a mini-infill session every 2 to 3 weeks to maintain full weightless volume.

Do I need to place an upfront deposit to secure an appointment slot?

Yes. To respect our clean-window scheduled time frames, we implement a standard 40% commitment down payment for specialized bridal glams and extensive treatments. The outstanding balance is cleared immediately after the service.

Are home visits or corporate off-site services available?

Absolutely. While our private velvet oasis studio offers the best luxury gear environment, off-site packages for large bridal groups, media campaigns, or events can be explicitly requested through our active WhatsApp routing link.
